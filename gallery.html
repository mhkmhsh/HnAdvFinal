Commonly Asked Questions!

Can you tell us about your companys history and mission?
Our company was founded in 1995 with the mission to inspire creativity and joy in children through innovative and high-quality toys. We aim to create products that not only entertain but also educate and foster imagination.

Who is your target market for your products?
Our target market primarily includes children aged 3-12 and their parents. We also cater to educators and gift buyers looking for quality, educational toys.

What are your most recent innovations or new product lines?
Recently, we introduced the "EcoPlay" line, which features toys made from sustainable and recycled materials. Additionally, we've launched a series of augmented reality (AR) enabled toys that blend physical and digital play.
